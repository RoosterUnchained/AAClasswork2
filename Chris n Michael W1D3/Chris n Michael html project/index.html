Description

This recipe will produce the greatest and tastiest egg omelette you ever had!!! Check out the links below for further info.

Egg Omelette Wiki

Omelette Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Onions
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Salt/Pepper

Cooking Directions
  1. Heat pan with oil
  2. Cut up all vegetables
  3. Whisk the 2 eggs together until fluffy
  4. Cook vegetables in pan for 5 minutes
  5. Then, pour in the whisked eggs
  6. Sprinkle in salt & pepper
  7. Cook until eggs they are done
  8. Serve and enjoy
